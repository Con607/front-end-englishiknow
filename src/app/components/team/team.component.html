English I Know Team

Elizabeth Anderson

Author

Started traveling to Guatemala and Mexico in 2002. During her travels she realized that often times the schools didn't have comprehensible material to learn the English language with. Most all schools in Mexico and Guatemala require English as a subject but very few come out knowing any real English. After months of creating, writing, and revising with various school teachers in Guatemala she published her first Conozco Ingles book to the public. She now works diligently to provide her materials to public schools in Mexico, Guatemala and individuals interested in learning English. Elizabeth teaches group classes in the Quetzaltenango area to adults and kids still in school. Her dream is to provide a solid way for Spanish speakers to learn English without a lot of confusion. 

Chris Anderson

Engineer and Computer Programmer

Chris and Coleen Anderson developed the learning system used in Conozco Ingles many years ago to help their children advance in school. When they started traveling in 2002 none of their kids knew Spanish. Chris created a computer program to help his children learn the language. All six of his children learned Spanish and continue to speak it fluently to this day.
Chris Anderson as an Engineer and his wife, Coleen Anderson as an artist and editor, have contributed to the Conozco Ingles program all through it's development.
